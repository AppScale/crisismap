Welcome to Google Crisis Map BETA

Google Crisis Map is an open source tool from Google Crisis Response that you can use to create and publish map "mashups" for crisis, humanitarian, and social good purposes.
A map "mashup" combines layers of map data from around the web. You can use many kinds of data with Google Crisis Map, including KML files, GeoRSS feeds, tile servers, Google My Maps, Google Maps Engine, and Google Fusion Tables. To get started, see:
  • Introductory video
  • Help documentation
  • Examples of maps by the Google Crisis Response team
Google Crisis Map is in beta, so it may have a few rough edges. Please use the Feedback link in the top-right corner to let us know how it works for you. Thanks!
